Welcome To My Personal GitHub Page

My name is Joshua Barretto (Barry Smith and Zesterer are my online aliases). I am a software developer that usually works in C++, Python, Vala or D. My projects cover a relatively big array of subjects. I've built games, simulators, libraries, physics engines, raytracers, text editors, emulators, chess AI and web browsers. Feel free to take a look through my work if you want. I usually license my work under GPL2, so it's totally fine for you to modify, copy and use most of my work freely. Although, of course, I'd prefer you gave me credit for it when you do.
